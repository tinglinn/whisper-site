About

9.8% of children and 4% of adults in the United States are diagnosed with ADHD. Those with ADHD often struggle with executive functioning due to time blindness and decision paralysis. This negatively affects their ability to manage their schedule.

We want to help. Whisper is a mobile application that helps people with ADHD combat time blindness and cultivate mindfulness through task and time visualization. Our mission is to help our users build a better relationship with time.

Team

We have a cross-functional team of 4, with each member taking one or more roles of designer, developer and product manager.

Design Process

We applied the full design-thinking cycle, including iterations of ideating, prototyping and evaluation. Our core values for the design were friendly, intuitive and enjoyable.
